About Me

I am Tuto Music — a sound engineer driven by perception, emotion, and musical intention rather than technical complexity. I understand what makes a record feel alive.

My work lives at the intersection of raw, urban energy and clean, high-end sonics. Whether it's the weight of an 808 or the intimacy of a vocal, every decision I make serves the emotion of the song.
